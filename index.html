how it works

Your NFT journey in 4 simple steps

01

Create your free NFT avatar

If you want to start a journey in our NFT world, the first step is to create your own Avatar NFT, which is totally free and is like your ID cards picture, after Avatar is created, it is ready to be sent in your Fibart wallet, after that, it can't be sent from the wallet, so NFT Avatar and wallet will be ONE forever.

02

Buy NFT art

After creating Avatar and transfering it to the Fibart wallet, now it's possible to buy any Fibart NFT with the wallet, you can buy how much NFT you want, but you can use just 1 NFT for the next step, which makes Fibart NFT's speacial.

03

Get digital NFT display screen

Surprise! You can buy Digital screen too. You can have just 1 NFT screen in your wallet, screens are everywhere in most cities, so choose which NFT you want and show it to the whole city.

04

Show your NFT to everyone

If you connected your NFT and screen to eachother, now it is called screeNFT, your NFT will be shown on the screen for 5 minutes in every hour, so your NFT can become popular.
